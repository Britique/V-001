About Us

Expert Careers and Employability Support Tailored for You

Our team of Education professionals have over 20 years of experience in delivering effective careers information, advice, guidance and employability skills services to a wide range of customers including those experiencing short and long term unemployment, at risk of redundancy, or currently excluded from the labour market due to disability or Special educational needs. Briteque focuses on innovation and excellence in the delivery of its customised employability training and careers advice programmes. We strive for positive outcomes for our candidates at every turn.
